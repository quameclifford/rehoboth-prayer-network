About the Ministry

Rehoboth Prayer Network is a prophetic prayer ministry devoted to raising a global altar of prayer through intercession, spiritual counsel, biblical teaching, and revival gatherings.

Through Global Dawn Prayers, daily prayer bulletins, counseling support, and strategic prayer gatherings, the ministry serves individuals, families, workers, and leaders across nations.
Vision
To raise a global altar of prayer where lives are transformed, destinies are restored, and nations are impacted through intercession.
Mission
To build a prayer-centered community through daily intercession, biblical teaching, prophetic ministry, counseling, and revival programs.
